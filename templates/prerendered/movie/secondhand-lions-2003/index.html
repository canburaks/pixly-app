Synopsis

The film tells the story of an introverted young (Haley Joel Osment) who is sent to live with his eccentric great-uncles (R. Duvall and M. Caine) on a farm in Texas. A boy named Walter is dropped by his mother Mae at his great-uncles' house. Later,Walter will find out his great-uncles' big secret. And rumors say that Hub & Garth, Walter's great uncles, have stolen much gold & money. (some say they stole it from Al Capone) Did they really steal that money or not?
