Golden Bear Awards

The Golden Bear (German: Goldener Bar) is the highest prize awarded for the best film at the Berlin International Film Festival. The bear is the heraldic animal of Berlin, featured on both the coat of arms and flag of Berlin. Here we offer you the list ''All Golden Bear winners from 1951 to 2019''.
