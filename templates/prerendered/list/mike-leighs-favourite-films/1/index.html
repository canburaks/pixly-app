Director Mike Leigh's Favourite Films

Michael "Mike" Leigh, is a British writer and director. Because studying theatre at the Royal Academy, He began his career as theatre director. One of the most well-known work of him is Naked (1993). He won the Best Director Award at Cannes. Besides, He won the Palm d'Or with Secrets & Lies (1996) film. In 2012, Mike Leigh made his favourite film list for Sight & Sound magazine. Here the favourite films of Mike Leigh.
