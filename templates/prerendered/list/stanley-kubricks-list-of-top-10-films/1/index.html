Stanley Kubrick's List of Top 10 Films

Nick Wrigley explores Kubrick's favorite films with the help of Kubrick's right-hand man Jan Harlan. The only Top 10 list that is known of Kubrick best movies submitted to anyone was in 1963 to a fledgling American magazine named Cinema . As Harlan told “Stanley would have seriously revised this 1963 list in later years, though Wild Strawberries, Citizen Kane and City Lights would remain, but he liked Kenneth Branagh’s Henry V much better than the old and old-fashioned Olivier version.”.
