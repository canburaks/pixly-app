BIOGRAPHY

Virginia Madsen (born September 11, 1961 height 5' 5" (1,65 m)) is an American actress and documentary film producer. She came to fame during the 1980s, having appeared in several films aimed at a teenage audience. Two decades later, she had an Academy Award and Golden Globe-nominated role in the 2004 film Sideways.
