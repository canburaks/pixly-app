Language is a part of our organism and no less complicated than it.

Biopic is the abbreviation for a biographical motion picture which stands for a movie that dramatizes the life or the important years of a real person. In biographical movies, the performance of the person who portrays the real figure is very important. Besides the acting skills, the physical appearance of an actor/actress should be also similar to the real person. Here the details of the best biography movies about the great historical figures.

 

Since we cannot list every biography film, we have created this film list considering the criteria below. The person who was portrayed in the movie (real person) can be one of the followings; 

  • The leader of a movement, community, nation or country at some point.
  • A controversial public figure that causes public disagreement.
  • An artist who has a global or regional reputation.
  • An intellectual, academic, scientist, writer or thinker.
  • A sportsperson who has achieved great success in his field.
