The Great Movies That Focus on Rich Dialogues

Michelangelo? You know a lot about him. Life's work, political aspirations. Him and the pope.Sexual orientation.The whole works, right? I bet you can't tell me what it smells like in the Sistine Chapel. You never actually stood there and looked up at that beautiful ceiling.Seeing that. If I ask you about women, you'll probably give me a syllabus of your personal favorites. You may have even been laid a few times. But you can't tell me what it feels like to wake up next to a woman... and feel truly happy.Sean McGuire (Good Will Huning, 1997)

Cinema is also a verbal tool as well as a visual one. Some films come to the fore with their rich dialogues without compromising their visual and audio values.There are even some that a whole film is almost exclusively among people in a room. Sometimes the dialogue itself can be very striking, and sometimes dialogues step by step forms a great narrative.

We collected movies that that focus primarily on rich dialogues. In Perfect Strangers, friendship of old friends are reviewed. In God on Trial ,God will be 'judged' in aconcentrations camp by the prisoners. In The trilogy ofBefore Sunrise, Before Sunset and Before Midnightwhich are filmed in20 years, we witness the dialogue between a woman and a man. In The Seventh Seal, we follow the conversation between a knight and the Death who came for him, and the existentialist perspective of Ingmar Bergman. 12 Angry Men have an 8.9IMDb points examined crime, punishment and prejudice and becomes one of the masterpieces in the history of cinema.
