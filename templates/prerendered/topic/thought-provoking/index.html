As the world's most complex art form, cinema offers us new ideas, new ways of thinking about something. Although it is very important to gaining a new ideas, the more important thing is to rethink what we assume is we already know.

The thought provoking movies that we curate for you examine thoroughly the questions that we have never thought of or paid enough attention to. The list has includes various provoking movies that will astonish you with a wide variety of interesting topics like time travel, mental illness, falling in love with an unexpected attitudes.

Let's look some of those mind bending movies briefly.

The Matrix;The best known work of theWachowski's, which has benefited much from the literature of philosophy and questioned reality itself from a descartesian point of view, and also touched on the dilemma of freedom and anthropogenic attitude of humanity towards everythings.

12 Angry Men;A courtroom story on the basis of reasonable doubt, justice system and the moral values.

Fight Club; ADavid Finchermovie that based on theChuck Palachniuk's novel questions the value system of advertising, indoctrinated values of capitalist production mechanisms and the conflicts of them with the Generation X.

Interstellar;Christopher Nolan's science-fiction movie is in complete harmony with the science of physics that leads us to rethink the terms time, space, space-time and relativity.

Her:Spike Jonze's sci-fi movie can be classified ascyberpunk. The movie cause us to rethink classical relationships in a very interesting way in the age of artificial intelligence is filling our needs.

The Experiment;Oliver Hirschbiegel's movie is based on a true story of shocking psychology experiment, known asStanford Prison Experiment, which was held on 1971 by a group of researcher led byProfessor Philip Zimbardo.The experiment itself revelas a wild side of human natura in scientific way and caused to start ethics discussions about the psychology experiments in reality.
